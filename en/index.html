THE 4 PRINCIPLES THAT GUIDE OUR CORPORATE GOVERNANCE POLICY:

4 PRINCIPLES

Promote transparency, accountability and empathy in managing relationships with stakeholders, to ensure our Clients are treated fairly and consistently;

To generate a positive impact on society, the economy and the environment through our activity;

Instill a culture of ethics and risk awareness;

Comply with high regulatory and governance standards, including those of the Standard Bank Group.
